The Tunnels - User Analysis

The Tunnels is an Internet based project by Robert Love which is being submitted to the School of Electronic and Temporal Arts at The University of Sydney's Sydney College of the Arts in partial fulfilment for the award of Master of Arts (Multimedia Design). The below questionnaire is intended to be completed after viewing The Tunnels Internet site at:
www.signified.com.au/thetunnels

All information provided will be kept strictly confidential. Your feedback is greatly appreciated.
